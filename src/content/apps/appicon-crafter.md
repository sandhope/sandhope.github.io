AppIcon Crafter - Professional App Icon Creation Made Easy

#### Overview

Are you looking for a powerful yet easy-to-use app icon creation tool? AppIcon Crafter transforms the process of creating app icons into a simple, enjoyable experience. Perfect for developers, designers, and anyone who wants professional-looking app icons for their projects.

#### Features

- **Multiple Icon Formats**: Support for SF Symbol, Emoji, PNG, SVG, and JPG formats. Browse and search through thousands of system icons with ease.
- **Powerful Icon Combination**: Combine multiple icon elements (SF Symbol + Emoji + Image + SVG) to create complex, professional app icons. Adjust position, size, and color of each element. Support layer management and transparency adjustment.
- **Flexible Color Settings**: Choose from solid colors or create beautiful gradients with 8 preset directions (left→right, right→left, top→bottom, bottom→top, top-left→bottom-right, top-right→bottom-left, bottom-left→top-right, bottom-right→top-left). Support for transparent, solid, and gradient background types. Support custom foreground and background colors.
- **Customizable Sizes**: Generate icons in sizes ranging from 64px to 4096px, perfect for all platforms and devices.
- **High-Quality Export**: Export in PNG format with full transparency support. Ensure crisp, clear icons on all devices.
- **Cross-Platform Support**: Create app icons for iOS, macOS, and other platforms from a single design.
- **Real-time Preview**: See your icon design come to life instantly with live preview functionality.
- **Intuitive Interface**: Modern SwiftUI-based interface designed for ease of use, no design experience required.
- **Dark Mode Support**: Full dark mode support for comfortable working in any lighting condition.

#### Data Privacy and Security

- AppIcon Crafter is an offline-only application that processes all data locally on your device.
- No internet connection is required, and no icon data is uploaded to the internet or cloud services.
- We promise not to collect, store, or share any of your icon designs or personal data.
- All your icon creations remain confidential and secure on your local device.

#### Is the app native?

Yes, it's native and written in Swift and SwiftUI for optimal performance and macOS integration.

#### Why is this free without ads?

We believe in providing a pure icon creation experience without distractions. AppIcon Crafter is designed to help developers and designers create professional app icons efficiently, not on ads or in-app purchases.

#### Can you localize the app into my language?

Currently, AppIcon Crafter supports English and Chinese. We are open to adding more languages based on user requests.
