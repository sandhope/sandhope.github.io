Rusty Pen - Where Old Ink Meets New Ideas

#### Overview

Are you looking for a writing application that combines the charm of traditional writing tools with modern digital convenience? Rusty Pen transforms writing into a ritual experience, perfect for writers, journal enthusiasts, and anyone who craves distraction-free creativity.

#### Features

- **Immersive Writing Experience**: Choose from 4 unique pen types (fountain pen, brush pen, feather pen, ballpoint pen), each with distinct font styles and writing sound effects.
- **Vintage Paper Themes**: Select from 4 paper themes (vintage stationery, parchment, manuscript paper, telegram paper) or upload custom backgrounds.
- **Personalized Environment**: Adjust paper transparency, use A4 aspect ratio, and choose from multiple fonts and sizes.
- **Atmospheric Sound System**: Enhance your writing with ambient sound effects (fireplace, rain, train, cafe, forest, ocean waves) and customizable pen/button sounds.
- **Beautiful Visual Themes**: Switch between 6 global themes (light, dark, autumn falling leaves, winter snowy night, ink style, vintage) with elegant animations.
- **Efficient Article Management**: Create, view, and organize articles with auto-save functionality and persistent local storage.
- **PDF Export**: Preserve your work with PDF export, maintaining all paper themes and styles.
- **International Support**: Full English and Chinese interface localization.
- **Secure and Private**: Offline-only application with local storage, ensuring your writing remains private and secure.

#### Data Privacy and Security

- Rusty Pen is an offline-only application that stores all data locally on your device.
- No internet connection is required, and no data is uploaded to the internet or cloud services.
- We promise not to collect, store, or share any of your personal data or writing content.
- All your writing remains confidential and secure on your local device.

#### Is the app native?

Yes, it's native and written in Swift and SwiftUI.

#### Why is this free without ads?

We believe in providing a pure writing experience without distractions. Rusty Pen is designed to help writers focus on their craft, not on ads or in-app purchases.

#### Can you localize the app into my language?

Currently, Rusty Pen supports English and Chinese. We are open to adding more languages based on user requests.
